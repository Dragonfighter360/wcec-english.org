VBS 2014

July14-18, 2014
VBS  9:00am–3:00pm
Child Care 3:00-5:00pm

 

@1512 Brackenville Rd, Hockessin, DE19707

302-239-4990 ‧ www.wcec-home.org

 

“Son Treasure Island” for Children Ages 4 to 12

Kids will discover God’s forever love when they learn about 1 Corinthians 13. 

VBS includes learning Bible verses, games, crafts, awesome music, tasty snacks, 

fun skits and much more. Join us at Son Treasure Island where kids find

the treasure of God’s love illustrated by the life of Jesus. 

Please Register by July 6 

Make checks payable to WCEC. Each child will receive a T-shirt, sunglasses, a music CD, connection cards, & stickers. Lunch is not provided. 

For mail-in registration, please send to WCEC and attention to VBS.

Suggest donation: $40 per child
