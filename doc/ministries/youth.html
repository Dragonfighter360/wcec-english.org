Mentoring

NEW Mentoring Program

Starting very soon, each young person in grade 7-9 will have an older youth as a mentor. Mentors will work with the younger students to try to help them fit in to the youth group, learn how to participate in youth group activities, and learn some basics about how to grow spiritual.

 

The mentoring program is new, experimental and voluntary. No one is being forced to be mentored or to mentor a younger student. We are just trying to develop an ongoing system of older students making intentional efforts to befriend a younger student and give them ideas on how to grow spiritually.

 

If you have any questions, please contact Pastor Brian.

24 Hour Counselor
