Cross Cultural Ministry


The Cross Cultural Couples Fellowship is a group of very diverse and interesting people that meet together at various times of the month for dinners, activities and discussions about issues that are important to the group. We currently meet monthly for a pot luck dinner and discussion on parenting issues.
