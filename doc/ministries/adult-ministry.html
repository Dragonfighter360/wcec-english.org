Community Life Bible Study

Community Life Bible Studies include both Sunday School for Adults as well as meetings during the weekday. 

Currently, we only have 1 CL Bible study that meets in Greenville. Click on the map below for directions. 

 

What to Expect:

The material used for the weekday CL Bible Studies varies but it will usually be a discussion driven video, book or topic that is of interest to those participating. Discussions are casual and participants are encouraged to simple share their thoughts about the topic at hand. 

You'll find a very accepting and friendly environment at our Community Life Bible Study.

Childcare is currently not provided but we are working on some solutions for this in the near future.  

Our main method of communication about group meetings, activities and extra conversations will be through a Google Group. You can join this group by clicking the logo below:
