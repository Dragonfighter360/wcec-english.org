WELCOME

Wilmington Community Evangelical Church is a non-denominational, multi-cultural church located at the corner of Limestone Rd. (Rt. 7) and Brackenville Rd. in Hockessin, Delaware.

We are a community of mostly Chinese believers but we also have a very active English Ministry geared for all ages.

The church family at WCEC would welcome your visit and we're sure that you'll be greatly warmly as you discover what we have to offer for every member of your family.
